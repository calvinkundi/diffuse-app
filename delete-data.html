Data Deletion — Diffuse

Diffuse is a mental wellbeing app developed by Calvin Kundi.
Last updated: 16 May 2026

This page explains how to delete your data from Diffuse, what data is deleted, and what (if anything) is retained.

How to delete your data from within the app

  1. Open the Diffuse app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (top right of the home screen).
  3. Scroll down to Clear All Data.
  4. Tap Clear All Data, then confirm when prompted.

This action takes effect immediately and cannot be undone.

What is deleted

When you tap "Clear All Data," Diffuse permanently removes from your device:

  • All journal entries (brain dump and reflection content)
  • All saved inspirational quotes you've favourited
  • Your full session history and journey records
  • All app settings, preferences, and consent toggles (including your AI feature opt-in)
  • All locally stored encrypted data

Because Diffuse stores most user data exclusively on your device, the "Clear All Data" action removes it entirely with no backups retained.

What may be briefly retained outside the app

Diffuse uses two third-party services that may briefly hold limited, anonymous data:

  • Firebase Crashlytics — if the app has ever crashed on your device, an anonymous crash report (containing stack trace, device model, and Android version, but no personal data or app content) is sent to Google. Crashlytics automatically deletes these reports after 90 days. No action is needed from you, and no per-user deletion is possible because the reports are anonymous.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — only if you have opted into the AI reflection feature and have actively requested at least one reflection. In that case, the text you sent for that reflection may be retained by Anthropic for up to 30 days for safety review, then permanently deleted. No personal identifiers are sent with the text.

If you have never enabled AI features, no data has ever been sent to Anthropic.

Uninstalling the app

If you uninstall Diffuse without first using "Clear All Data," your local data is removed by the Android operating system as part of normal uninstall behaviour. Any anonymous crash reports already sent to Crashlytics will still expire after 90 days as described above.

Note: Diffuse does not require you to create an account, sign in, or share any personal information. We do not maintain any user database. The information above describes all the data flows that exist; nothing else about you is collected or stored.
