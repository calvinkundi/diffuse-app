Privacy Policy

Diffuse — Your companion for overwhelming moments

🔒 Zero data collection

The Simple Version

Diffuse does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no way to see what you do in the app.

What Data Exists

Diffuse stores the following information locally on your device to provide its features:

  • Your app preferences and settings (dark mode, sound, haptic intensity, breathing duration)
  • Session history (completion count, streaks, timestamps)
  • Brain dump journal entries you choose to save
  • Stress level check-in responses

This data is stored using your device's secure storage (encrypted) and shared preferences. It never leaves your device.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, location)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent to external services
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

No Accounts Required

Diffuse does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. There is no registration process.

No Network Communication

Diffuse does not communicate with any servers. The app functions entirely offline. The only network activity occurs if you choose to:

  • Tap a crisis helpline link (opens your phone dialer or browser)
  • Tap the email contact link (opens your email app)
  • Share the app with friends (opens your device's share sheet)
  • View the Privacy Policy or Terms of Service (opens your browser to this page)

These actions are initiated by you and handled by your device's operating system, not by Diffuse.

Third-Party Services

Diffuse does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app contains no trackers of any kind.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, which have their own privacy policies and may collect data related to app downloads and installation. This is outside of Diffuse's control.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Flutter Secure Storage (encrypted, for sensitive data like journal entries)
  • Shared Preferences (for app settings and non-sensitive preferences)

You can delete all stored data at any time through the app's Settings page using "Clear Progress". Uninstalling the app will also remove all data.

Children's Privacy

Diffuse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no data from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Mental Health Disclaimer

Diffuse is a wellness tool designed to help manage everyday stress and anxiety. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a substitute for professional mental health care.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm, or suicidal ideation, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately. Crisis resources are available within the app.

International Users

Diffuse is available worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since Diffuse collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
